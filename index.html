What Is APPLE Company?

Apple is an American company that manufactures and manufactures computer software products. The most popular products are iPod, iPhone, and computer hardware. The Apple software includes the Mac OS X operating system, the iTunes media browser, the iLife group for multimedia software, creative software, and the iWork group for productivity programs.

Apple was founded in 1976, and it is an American company. Steve Wozniak, Ronald Wayne and Steve Jobs are among the founders of the company and won an award (National Design Awards).
